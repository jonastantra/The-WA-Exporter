Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work? +
The extension automatically scrolls through your contact list and safely extracts visible information like names and phone numbers.
Is it safe? +
Yes, the extraction happens locally on your computer. We do not store your contacts on our servers.
Why did it stop? +
If the internet connection is slow or the list ends, the process stops automatically. You can restart it anytime.
How to export to iPhone? +
Select the "VCard" format. This generates a .vcf file that you can email to yourself and open on your iPhone to import all contacts at once.
